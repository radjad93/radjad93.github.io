FIFTH SPHERE EXPANSION

Fifth Sphere Expansion is a Warhammer 40000 Battle Report in the form of a zine. A battle report is a turn-by-turn account of battles of Warhammer 40000, a tabletop war game played by two or more players. This zine was created in the form that appears in the game's official magazine, which includes miniature photography, illustrated maps and formatted army lists.

Jared Wee 2018
